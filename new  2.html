Local Fair Coordinator Instructions for Registering for the NIRSEF - REQUIRED

Please read through the following information about registering your school and student projects for the NIRSEF. If you have any questions, you may contact the NIRSEF Executive Director, Alisa Zornig.

  1. 1. Register your school at http://www.sefireg.org/.
    • Returning Local Fair Coordinators will select the "Teacher Login" button on the SEFI website and reactivate their accounts by logging in with their school email address and password from the last year they sent students (they may also retrieve their password online). 
    • New Local Fair Coordinators will select the "New Teacher Registration" on the SEFI website, select their school, and complete their information.
  2. Set up your student accounts by entering each participant's name and entering their project information. You will enter all students who participated in your local fair first and then designate those who are advancing to the NIRSEF after you hold your local school fair. All project information must be submitted at the SEFI site NO LATER THAN 5:00 pm FRIDAY, March 7, 2014. NO projects will be allowed to enter the NIRSEF after this date!
  3. If your school is paying the $10 per student registration fee, please submit it online and advise your students to select ‘my school is paying my fee’ when they fill out the online Availability to Participate and Payment Method. If your school does not allow online payment by debit, credit, or electronic check, please contact the NIRSEF Executive Director, Alisa Zornig.
  4. ALL projects dealing with the following materials MUST sumbit review paperwork to the NIRSEF Safety Review Comittee (SRC) BEFORE experimentation begins (check if a project requires pre-approval).Projects dealing with the following MUST be done in a BSL-2 Lab under supervision. 
    • microorganisms (e.g. bacteria)
    • rRDNA
    • Tissues ofr bodily fluids
  5. Projects dealing with Human Participants MUST submit review paperwork to an Institutional Review Board before (IRB)experimentation begins. A school may set up an IRB according to these guidelines.
  6. All projects must complete the SRC/IRB paperwork: Form 1, Form 1A and Form 1B. Projects requiring prior approval must complete additional forms and submit to the NIRSEF SRC or school IRB (as defined above). 
  7. Number of Project spaces per school at the NIRSEF (individual OR team):
    • a. Schools with Grades K-4 or 7-8: 4 project spaces
    • b. Schools with Grades 5-8 or 6-8: 6 project spaces
    • c. Schools with Grades K-8: 8 project spaces
    • d. All high schools: 10 project spaces
      • You may request additional project spaces by contacting the Executive Director.
    • Once registered, teachers who want to check on their students' progress in entering project information can login at http://www.sefireg.org/ and do so. Click on "Student Listing" and you will see a list of your students. When you click on "View", you will can check on each section of your student's information.
